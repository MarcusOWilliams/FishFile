Searching the database

There are two main ways to search for an entry in the database: The simplest way is to use the small search bar in the top right hand corner, this allows you to search for a specific fish tank by giving its stock number If you want a more detailed search you can use the search form on the home page, this will allow you to filter by a number of attributes of any entry and will return a list of all the entries that match your search, but searching with none of the fields filled in you can get a list of every fish entry in the database

Creating an Excel Spreadsheet From the Database

DanioDB gives you the ability to create a quick an easy excel spreadsheet from database entries, all you need to do is copy the data you require from the website and format it in an excel spreadsheet by following the steps below.

Step 1:
Find the data you want to create your spreadsheet from, this can be found at the bottom of your search results, which will contain the information of all the entries that match the search or at the bottom of the page for an individual fish entry. Once you have filtered to the information you require, press the copy to clipboard button.

Step 2:
Open a new excel document and paste the text into the first cell.

Then click on "A" at the top of the first column to highlight the entire column.

Step 3:
Select the data tab at the top of the screen.

In the data tools section, select text to columns.

Step 4:
A popup should appear, on the first screen cahnge the selected field from "Fixed width" to "Delimited".

Press next, on the next screen select the box next to "Comma".

Finally you should be able to press finish and the spreadsheet shuold be correctly formatted.
